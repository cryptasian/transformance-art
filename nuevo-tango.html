Nuevo Tango

Buy Tickets

TICKETS

 $30 (Standard)
 $50 (VIP)

WHEN

April 16, 2023 | 6-9:30pm

WHERE

Fourth United Presbyterian Church
1323 N. Broadway, Knoxville, TN 37917

Join us for a celebration of the Tango-inspired compositions of the exceptional Argentinian composer, Astor Piazzolla. Immerse yourself in an evening of beautiful Chamber Music, Tango dances accompanied by live music, and a Social Dance Party. This is truly a one-of-a-kind Chamber Music concert, where two art forms collide to create an experience unlike any other.

Chamber Music

KnoxTrio (Joshua Ulrich: Violin, Sarah Senn: Cello and Belle Toh: Piano) will perform Bordel 1900, Cafe 1930, Nightclub 1960, Escualo, Oblivion, Libertango and Four Seasons of Buenos Aires.

Dance

Kris Hazard and Natalie Thompson will perform a tango fusion to Escualo and Libertango.

Argentine Tango Society will improvise an Argentine Tango to Oblivion.

Social Dance Party

Argentine Tango Society will teach a short Argentine Tango lesson.

The social dance party will be hosted by Marble City Ballroom.
