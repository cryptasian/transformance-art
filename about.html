Who We Are

Mission: Performance that Transforms
Transformance Art is an organization dedicated to produce exceptional non-traditional classical chamber concerts where professional musicians, dancers, and visual artists work together by merging two or more forms of art to communicate sounds, movements and visuals in new and creative ways.

Vision
Our vision is to uplift, inspire and restore the community through excellent art that is presented in a way that the viewer’s are involved in some way, and where artists are free to be creative with their skills and talents.
