Transformance Art is ...

Creative expression has been shown to reduce stress, build emotional resilience, and promote healing. But the benefits extend beyond the artist - research confirms that simply experiencing the arts can ease symptoms of depression and strengthen well-being. At Transformance Art, we empower artists to heal through their work and bring transformative arts experiences to the community.

Projects

Meraki [may-rah-kee] is a Greek word that means "to do something with soul, creativity, or love; to put something of yourself in your work".

The Meraki Projects will be a series of relevant fine arts experiences that will promote hope, healing, and restoration. Each project will have a specific focus with the goal of bringing the experiences to cities around the world.

The first project will focus on suicide survival and healing.
