Past Events

Regency Soirée | Nov. 10, 2024

A Bridgerton-inspired fundraiser ball that featured live music and dance performances, a dance lesson (provided by Marble City Ballroom), signature cocktails, light hors d'oeuvres and a silent auction.

The proceeds of this event are dedicated to the development of our 2025 project "Strength and Survival: Creative Expressions of Suicide and Loss".

Nuevo Tango | April 16, 2023

A celebration of the Tango-inspired compositions of the exceptional Argentinian composer, Astor Piazzolla. Participants were immersed in an evening of beautiful Chamber Music, Tango dances accompanied by live music, and a Social Dance Party. This was truly a one-of-a-kind Chamber Music concert, where two art forms collided to create an experience unlike any other.

The proceeds of this event covered the expenses for all the necessary filings to become a 501(c)(3) nonprofit recognized by the IRS!
