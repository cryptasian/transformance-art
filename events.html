Upcoming

In partnership with Marble City Ballroom

Dearest reader,

The social season is upon us, and whispers of an extraordinary event have reached my ears. Prepare to be captivated, for a Bridgerton Soirée of unparalleled grandeur awaits!

Dust off your finest attire, for this is an occasion to showcase your most exquisite period garb.

A dance lesson shall commence the festivities, ensuring even the most hesitant wallflower blossoms into a graceful swan upon the dance floor. As the night unfolds, enjoy a cash bar complete with two Signature Bridgerton Drinks, modernized Regency-era music, performances, and more!

Remember, dear reader, this soirée is not merely an opportunity to revel in the splendor of the era. It is a compassionate endeavor, a hand outstretched to those navigating the complexities of mental health. Your presence at this enchanting evening will directly contribute to the development of Transformance Art's upcoming Meraki Project, "Strength and Survival: Creative Expressions of Overcoming Suicide and Loss." Together, we shall cultivate an artistic haven where hope flickers, healing blossoms, and resilience takes root; a sanctuary for those touched by the shadows of depression, the weight of loss, and the journey towards recovery.

Secure your invitation posthaste, for this is an affair not to be missed. Let it be known that your presence at the Bridgerton Soirée shall be the talk of the ton, and a beacon of light in the darkness.

  • Dance the Night Away: Learn the steps to a Bridgerton-inspired dance, taught by Marble City Ballroom, and twirl across the dance floor to enchanting music

  • Silent Auction: Participate in a silent auction - with proceeds benefiting a worthy cause and some very exciting items to bid on (just in time for Christmas!)
  • Support a Worthy Cause: Proceeds benefit Transformance Art's Meraki Project, promoting mental health awareness and healing through the arts
